Soil Health Assessment

Understanding soil health is essential for sustainable agriculture and environmental protection.

This tool allows users to assess the condition of their soil based on key indicators such as nutrient content, moisture, pH levels, and other critical factors.

By analyzing your input, the model predicts overall soil health and offers insights that can guide effective soil management and crop planning.

Whether you're a farmer, researcher, or enthusiast, this platform helps you make informed decisions to enhance productivity and maintain ecological balance.
